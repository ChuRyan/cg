In my freshman year of high school, I was inspired by a 3D fan animation of the game, League of Legends. From there, I stumbled through multiple tutorials and created numerous independent projects in order to master the art of 3D Animation and share my own stories with the rest of the world. In terms of software, I am proficient with Blender, as well as the modeling tools in Autodesk Maya.

Structure


Base (w/ Marbles)


The Tech Challenge 2015 required participants to create a structure that would be earthquake- proof. To do this, I created a structure with a separated base that used marbles and elastic string to ensure the actual structure wouldn't be affected at all by the earthquake. For the structure, I used light cardboard and cut-up PCV pipes, which allow for flexibility.
