About Me

Ryan Chu is the president of a powerful nation, the Republic of Ryan, and the founder of the beautiful coastal city, Port Folio.

In reality though, I'm just a high school student living in Cupertino, California, not the president of a country. This website is my own little town in the vast universe of the Internet. Here, I will be posting various creations that I have done: games, art, and music. Some people say that I'm creative, but I believe everyone is creative; I just get inspired easily, and this motivation pushes me to create more and build more. I have an artist's eye and the technical skills of an engineer, allowing me to craft a variety of creations. Look around my portfolio, and I hope you enjoy your stay at Port Folio.
